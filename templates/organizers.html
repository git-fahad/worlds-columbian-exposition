The Organizers

Executive Leadership

HCA

Harlow Niles Higinbotham

President

As President of the World's Columbian Exposition, Higinbotham oversaw the entire operation, coordinating between government officials, business leaders, and international participants to ensure the fair's success.

DB

Daniel Burnham

Director of Works

Burnham was responsible for the architectural vision and construction coordination of the entire fairgrounds. His leadership brought together the finest architects and artists of the era.

JW

John Wellborn Root

Chief of Construction

Root worked closely with Burnham to oversee the massive construction project, ensuring that the architectural vision was realized within the tight timeline and budget constraints.

JN

John Newhouse

Secretary

Newhouse managed the administrative aspects of the exposition, handling correspondence, documentation, and coordination with participating nations and exhibitors.

Board of Directors

The Board of Directors consisted of prominent business leaders, civic officials, and philanthropists who provided strategic oversight and financial backing for the exposition.

Board of Lady Managers

BP

Bertha Honoré Palmer

President, Board of Lady Managers

The Board of Lady Managers was a groundbreaking organization that ensured women's participation in the exposition. Led by Bertha Palmer, they oversaw the Woman's Building and advocated for women's representation in all aspects of the fair.
