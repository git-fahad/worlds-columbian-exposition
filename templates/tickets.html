Visitor Guidelines

Please observe decorum appropriate to this grand occasion. Prohibited items include outside food and beverages, animals (except service animals), and large parcels. First aid stations are clearly marked on the map.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to the fair? expand_more

The Illinois Central Railroad provides direct service to the fairgrounds. Streetcars and horse-drawn omnibuses are also available from all parts of Chicago. Water taxis operate along the lakefront for a scenic journey.

Is the fair accessible for persons with disabilities? expand_more

Yes, rolling chairs are available for rental for visitors requiring mobility assistance. Most major walkways and buildings are designed to be accessible.

What dining options are available? expand_more

A wide variety of restaurants, cafes, and refreshment stands are located throughout the fairgrounds, offering cuisine from around the world to suit every taste and budget.

Are photographs permitted? expand_more

Personal photography for non-commercial use is permitted. However, the use of large tripods may be restricted in crowded areas or certain exhibits. Please be considerate of other visitors.
